Privacy Policy

This page explains how the Salise Ligi web application and mobile app approach user data. By using the application, you agree to the following terms.

1. Account & Login

Salise Ligi does not include any user account, login or registration system. No identity information such as name, email address, or password is collected or stored.

2. Data Collected

The application does not directly collect personal data. However, the following third-party services may process some anonymous data indirectly:

  • Google AdSense (Web): Google AdSense may be used for ad display in the web version. AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to serve interest-based ads.
  • Google AdMob (Mobile): AdMob may be used for ad display in the Android app. AdMob may process the device's advertising ID and anonymous usage data.
  • Basic Analytics: Basic analytics data (page views, error reports, etc.) may be collected anonymously to monitor app performance.

3. Cookies

Salise Ligi does not create cookies itself. However, third-party advertising and analytics providers (Google AdSense, Google Analytics, etc.) may place their own cookies. These cookies are used for:

  • Personalized ad delivery
  • Ad performance measurement
  • Anonymous usage statistics

You can manage or disable cookies from your browser settings. For Google's ad cookie preferences: Google Ad Settings.

4. Data Sharing & Sale

User data is not sold, rented, or shared commercially with third parties. Data is processed only within the scope of advertising and analytics services, in accordance with the respective providers' privacy policies.

5. Children's Privacy

Salise Ligi does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If parents or guardians believe a child has provided personal information, they may contact us.

6. Third-Party Links

The application may contain links to Google Play Store and other external sites. Salise Ligi is not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites; we recommend reviewing their own privacy policies.

7. Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through this page. We recommend checking the current version regularly.
