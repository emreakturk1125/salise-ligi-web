How to Play Salise Ligi?

Salise Ligi is a fast-paced football and reflex game you can play for free in your browser. No download needed — open your browser, tap the ball and start the match!

Basic Controls

The game mechanics are very simple:

  1. Tap the ball — The turn starts, the ball spins and the number in the balloon rapidly cycles 0–9.
  2. Tap again — You stop the ball. The number you catch determines the outcome (goal, penalty, card, offside, etc.).
  3. The time bar at the top shows remaining time. Stop before it runs out; otherwise the ball stops automatically.
Each number has a different meaning: goal, offside, yellow card, red card, penalty and more. Check the in-game guide for details!

Game Modes

1. vs Bot (Single Player)

Play alone against the AI opponent. The bot makes automatic moves when it's their turn. Develop strategies, master your timing and beat the bot!

2. Same Device 2 Players

Take turns on the same phone or computer. After the first player finishes their turn, the second player goes next. Perfect for playing side by side with a friend.

Penalties & End of Match

During the match, the team that catches number 7 wins a penalty. You need to tap the ball again for the penalty kick. Even numbers (0, 2, 4, 6, 8) count as goals; odd numbers (1, 3, 5, 7, 9) are missed.

If the match ends in a draw, it goes to penalty shootout: each team takes 5 shots. If still tied, "Golden Goal" (Sudden Death) kicks in — the first team to score wins.

Card System & Advantage

  • Yellow Card: Accumulates during the match. A second yellow automatically becomes a red card.
  • Red Card: Gives the opponent +1 extra move (advantage). Yellow cards reset after a red card.
  • Advantage System (+1): The advantaged team gets 2 consecutive moves. If they receive a red during their advantage turn, the advantage is cancelled or transferred.
  • Offside: The referee stops play, the turn is invalidated.

Tips

  1. Timing is everything: Stopping the ball too early or too late directly affects the outcome. Sharpen your reflexes.
  2. Calculate card risk: If you have a yellow, play cautiously; second yellow = red and advantage for the opponent.
  3. Aim for evens in penalties: Even numbers mean a goal; with good timing you can convert penalties.
  4. Learn the bot's pattern: In single-player mode, observe the bot's behavior and build your strategy accordingly.
  5. Adjust match duration: You can shorten or extend the match time in settings. Short matches = quick results, long matches = more strategic.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Salise Ligi free?
Yes, completely free. Play in your browser right away or download the Android app from Google Play.
Do I need to download anything?
Not for the web version. Open your browser and go to saliseligi.com, start playing immediately. For mobile, download from Google Play.
Which devices are supported?
Any device with a modern browser: phone, tablet, computer. Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge are supported.
How many languages are supported?
5 languages: Turkish, English, German, Spanish and Arabic. Change from in-game settings.
Can I change team names?
Yes! Customize both home and away team names from the pre-match settings screen.
Can I adjust match duration?
Yes, change the match duration from the settings menu as you like.
